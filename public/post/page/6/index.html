My latest toy - the Nokia N800

I picked up a new toy for myself for this last holiday season, a Nokia N800 Internet Tablet. It’s very cool. I bought it mainly to listen to streaming media and to keep up with e-mail and RSS feeds when not at my computer – which so far means lying in bed. The experience has been pretty good so far. I’ve moved all off my personal e-mail and feed reading to Google services, which offer plain XHTML interfaces if you know how to find them, and they work really well.
